PDE is a simple application that let you run Partial Differential Equations of the form $u_t(t,x,y) = f(t,x,y,u,u_x,u_y, u_{xx},u_{xy}, u_{yy})$ or the form $u_{tt}(t,x,y) = f(t,x,y,u,u_x,u_y, u_{xx},u_{xy}, u_{yy}, u_t)$, where $u_{xy} = \frac{\partial u}{\partial x\partial y}$.
Famous Equations are :
Heat Equation : $u_t = \Delta u = \text{div}(\nabla u) = u_{xx} + u_{yy}$
Wave Equation : $u_{tt} = \Delta u$
