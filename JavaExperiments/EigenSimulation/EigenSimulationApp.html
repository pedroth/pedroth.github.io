Eigen Simulation App is a application that visualize three algorithms of finding eigenvectors. For starters eigenvectors are vectors that have the following property:
let $A \in \mathbbb{R}^{n \times n}$ and $\mathbf{v} \in \mathbb{R}^n$ then $\mathbf{v}$ is a not trivial solution ( that means $\mathbf{v} \neq \mathbf{0}$) of : $$A\mathbf{v} = \lambda \mathbf{v}\;\;\;(1)$$ These eigenvectors and eigenvalues (the $\lambda$ 's in (1)) are very useful for different applications like Physics, Optimization, Machine Learning, etc. Here I explore methods to find eigenvectors. I tried three methods, the first is the power method and the other two are based in the next constraint optimization problem : A small detail I forgot to tell is that the matrix $A$ is symmetric (this is important because it gives guarantees about the existence of real eigenvectors and eigenvalues). $$\min_{\mathbf{x}\in \mathbb{R}^n} \left < \mathbf{x}, A \mathbf{x}\right >$$ $$\text{s.t } \;\;|\mathbf{x}|^2 = 1 \;\;\; (2)$$ Press "h" for help.
