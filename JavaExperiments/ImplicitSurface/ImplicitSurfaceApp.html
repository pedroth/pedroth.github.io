ImplicitSurface is a simple application where you can see surfaces that are the level sets of three dimensional functions. These surfaces are of the form $\left \{(x,y,z) \in \mathbb{R}^3 : f(x,y,z) = 0 \right \}$. For instance the sphere will have the following formula $x^2+y^2+z^2 -1 = 0$.
